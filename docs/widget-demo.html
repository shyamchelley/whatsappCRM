Embed on Your Website

Add the CRM widget to any webpage with 3 lines of code.

<!-- Step 1: Add this div where you want the widget mounted -->
<div id="crm-widget-root"
     data-api-base="http://localhost:3001"
     data-site-token="your_site_token"
     data-title="Get in Touch"
     data-subtitle="We reply within 1 hour"></div>

<!-- Step 2: Load the widget script -->
<script src="http://localhost:3001/widget/crm-widget.iife.js"></script>
⚙️

data-api-base

Your CRM backend URL. Change to your production domain when deploying.

🔑

data-site-token

Set SITE_TOKEN in your backend .env and use the same value here.

🎨

data-title / subtitle

Customize the widget heading and subtext to match your brand.
